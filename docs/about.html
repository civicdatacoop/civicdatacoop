About us

LCR Civic Data Cooperative

The Liverpool City Region Civic Data Cooperative is a team working under the Faculty of Health and Life Sciences at the University of Liverpool. We were established in spring 2021.

We are working on various projects mainly focused on health-related data. Our primary mission is to make data about citizens work for citizens. The LCR CDC is a growing team composed of experts.

What do we do?

We are a strong partner for projects that process data for helping people in Liverpool City Region.

Our LCR Civic Data Cooperative team has experience processing healthcare records and similar datasets to define actions that improve life in the region. We closely cooperate with all key stakeholders.

Contact Us Mission

We follow the best practices, document our solutions, and believe in transparency about our programme. Based on a collaborative, open approach, the LCR CDC supports people and organizations in their efforts.
