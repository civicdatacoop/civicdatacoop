About the CDC

Civic Data Cooperative team at the University of Liverpool

The Civic Data Cooperative (CDC) is a part of the Faculty of Health and Life Sciences at the University of Liverpool. We work on various projects that operate with data related to citizens helping to build a better region. When delivering our projects, we believe in a transparent, open approach following best practices. The CDC has access to various data, mainly related to healthcare, such as summary patient records or synthetic datasets.

  • Translating lived experience into insight.
  • Delivering innovation with impact.
  • Using health data ethically and inclusively.

CDC activities & projects

So far, we have participated in many projects that help people in Liverpool City Region. Our goal is to understand the problems of people, small businesses and organizations and find a way to deal with them effectively. We have organized actions like Citizens Jury, helping engage a wide public in finding solutions. Similarly, we managed CDC - what's your problem event.
