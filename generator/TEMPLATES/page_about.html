About us

LCR Civic Data Cooperative

The Liverpool City Region Civic Data Cooperative is a new type of data governance project, funded by the LIverpool City Region Combined Authority and hosted by the Faculty of Health and Life Sciences at the University of Liverpool.

We are working on various projects mainly focused on health-related data. Our primary mission is to enable positive change in health and wellbeing through data and cooperation.

What do we do?

We are working with stakeholders across the region, including NHS hospitals, local councils and others, to help a cooperative, data-sharing platform that enables better health and care for people everywhere.

Our LCR Civic Data Cooperative team has experience using healthcare records and civic datasets to enable research that can be used to support positive change for all our residents.
